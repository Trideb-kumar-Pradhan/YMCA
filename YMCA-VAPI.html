BOARD OF DIRECTORS

The Board of Directors of YMCA Vapi represents an optimal mix of professionalism, knowledge, and experience. The Board comprises eight members with three-year terms, with one-third of the members retiring annually, elected by the Full Members of the Association. The General Secretary is an ex-officio member of the Board. The Board Members also hold the Chairmanship of the various standing Committees of the YMCA.
